Guernsey Cycling Routes

View Route Table
A small island though it may be, Guernsey has a wealth of beautiful parishes and tranquil routes to explore, and in no better manner than by bike. Whether it is the steep inclines of Talbot Valley, the rolling fields of St Andrews, or the unforgettable views of a sunset over the sea, cycling in Guernsey is an adventure for riders of all ages and abilities.
