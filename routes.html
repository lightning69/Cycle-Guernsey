A small island though it may be, Guernsey has an enormous wealth of routes and areas to discover, and in no better manner than by bike. Whether it is the steep inclines of Talbot Valley, the rolling fields of St Andrews, or the urban distraction of the seafront which captures your imagination, the list of routes below contains all of these possibilities and more.
