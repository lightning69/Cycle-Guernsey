Guernsey Velo Club

The Guernsey Velo Club (GVC) was founded in 1968 and is one of the most adavnced and rigourous cycling clubs on the island. It boasts multiple different teams, each of which caters for a distinct type and level of cycling including mountain, road, racing, and endurance. The club hosts weekly races and endurance cycles Sunday mornings, open to all members, as well as a regular sequence of competitive events in cross country and road riding throughout the year.

To find out more, visit the GVC official website or contact President Mark Smith at markleesmith@yahoo.co.uk or 07839 264819.

Guernsey Rouleurs Cycling Club

Guernsey Rouleurs CC is a more relaxed and social cycling group than the Guernsey Velo Club, ideal for keen cyclists who don't enjoy the pressures of racing and extremely competitive cycling. Don't be fooled, though, the GRCC remains one of the most prolific cycling clubs on the island and has much to offer to excellent cyclists looking to challenge themselves. It operates mainly on Sunday mornings, hosting weekly rides between 6:30 and 8:30. Furthermore, the club offers cycling events with additional fun activities on a regular basis permitting riders to enjoy the friendly, social side of cycling and to meet other keen cyclists.

To find out more, visit the Guernsey Rouleurs CC official website or contact the club at guernseyrouleurs@gmail.com.

Major Events

Both cycling clubs host a comprehensive range of events for cyclists of all manner and ability on a regular basis. Whilst the GVC and Guernsey Rouleurs website do mention these events, they are not regularly updated and they do not provide a reliable list detailing events happening in the next few weeks. The best way to discover this information is to follow club social media or become a registered memeber.
