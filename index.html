Guernsey, a remote yet stunning island south of England and North of France, is a place to explore like no other. Famed for its natural beauty and tranquility, the secrets of the island are best discovered whilst cycling its many valleys, lanes, and seaside routes. This utopia is only made more appealing by its covid-free community with no need for restrictions, danger, or fear. Continue to our routes page, below, to find out more about the endless opportunites and places to discover on the island.


For keen cyclists wanting to move to Guernsey, or indeed living on the island already, there is a wealth of equipment to satisfy all needs. From top of the line racing bikes, to entry level mountain and road vehicles, shops such as Ian Browns and Adventure Cycles can provide everything you need. The availability and quality of accessories such as GPS bike trackers, clip-in pedals, and more, are all reviewed on our equipments page below.
